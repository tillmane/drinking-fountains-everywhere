Last updated: June 2026

Fountains for All is a free, volunteer-run community project based in Seattle, WA. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it. We collect as little as possible.

What we collect

Pilot sign-up form. If you request pilot access, we collect your email address. We use it only to contact you about the pilot program. It is not shared with anyone else or used for marketing.

In-app activity. When you rate a fountain or submit a report, we associate that action with an anonymous device ID generated by your browser. This lets us prevent duplicate ratings and track report history without requiring an account. We do not collect your name, location, or any identifying information as part of this.

Analytics. We use analytics software to understand general usage patterns — things like how many people visit, which pages they view, and what devices they use. We do not use analytics to track individuals. If and when we select an analytics provider, we'll update this policy with specifics.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell your data. Ever.
  • We don't run ads.
  • We don't require an account.
  • We don't knowingly collect data from anyone under 13.

Third-party services

Cloudflare. This site is served through Cloudflare, which acts as our hosting and security infrastructure. Cloudflare automatically collects certain technical data as part of standard web operations, including IP addresses, request headers, and HTTP access logs. This data is processed by Cloudflare in accordance with their Privacy Policy.

Google Forms. The pilot sign-up form is hosted by Google Forms and subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

OpenStreetMap. When you search for a location, your search query is sent directly to OpenStreetMap's servers and subject to the OpenStreetMap Privacy Policy. OpenStreetMap may receive your IP address as part of this request.

Seattle City GIS. Fountain location data is sourced from Seattle's open GIS datasets. This is a one-time data import — Seattle City GIS does not receive any user data through this site.

Data retention

Email addresses collected via the pilot form will be deleted once the pilot program ends or upon request. Anonymous device IDs and associated ratings/reports may be retained indefinitely to maintain map quality.

Your rights

If you want your email removed from our pilot list, or have any questions about your data, contact us at info@urbanfreerunners.com.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes — for instance, adding a named analytics provider — we'll update the date at the top of this page.
